# 📊 Supplier Analysis & Cost Optimization

## 📌 Problem
Companies that purchase products from multiple suppliers often face variations in pricing, delivery times, and consumption patterns, which can lead to inefficiencies and increased costs.

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze purchasing and consumption data to identify opportunities for cost optimization and improve supplier selection strategies.

## 📂 Data
- Purchase history  
- Supplier pricing  
- Customer consumption data  
- Order dates  

## 🔍 Analysis
- Price comparison across suppliers  
- Identification of the most cost-efficient suppliers  
- Customer consumption analysis  
- Detection of cost-saving opportunities  

## 📊 Results
- Identified suppliers offering lower average prices  
- Highlighted inefficiencies in purchasing patterns  
- Provided data-driven recommendations for supplier selection  

## 📈 Business Impact
This analysis enables companies to reduce costs, optimize procurement strategies, and make better data-driven decisions.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook

## 💡 Key Insights
- Supplier A offers the lowest average price for high-volume products  
- Supplier B shows price variability, indicating potential negotiation opportunities  
- Certain customers drive the majority of purchases, suggesting targeted optimization strategies  

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Consolidate purchases with cost-efficient suppliers  
- Negotiate pricing with high-variance suppliers  
- Optimize purchasing strategy based on consumption trends
